Popular Anime Series

Naruto is a famous anime about a young ninja named Naruto Uzumaki who dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. The series features characters like Sasuke Uchiha, who seeks revenge for his clan, and Sakura Haruno, a skilled medical ninja.

One Piece follows Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew searching for the legendary treasure called the One Piece. Luffy has eaten a Devil Fruit that gives him rubber powers but also prevents him from swimming. His crew includes Roronoa Zoro, a master swordsman, and Sanji, a powerful chef.

Attack on Titan takes place in a world where humanity lives inside massive walls to protect themselves from giant humanoid creatures called Titans. The main character, Eren Yeager, swears revenge after his mother is killed by a Titan. He later discovers he can transform into a Titan himself.

Demon Slayer follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is killed and his sister Nezuko is turned into a demon. The story features incredible sword fighting techniques called Breathing Styles, including Water Breathing and Sun Breathing.

My Hero Academia is set in a world where most people have superpowers called Quirks. Izuku Midoriya, born without a Quirk, inherits the powerful One For All from the hero All Might and trains at U.A. High School with classmates like Katsuki Bakugo, who has explosion powers, and Shoto Todoroki, who can control ice and fire.

Dragon Ball Z features Goku, a Saiyan warrior who protects Earth from powerful villains like Vegeta, Frieza, and Cell. The series introduced iconic transformations like Super Saiyan, where a Saiyan's hair turns gold and their power increases dramatically.

Death Note follows Light Yagami, a genius student who finds a notebook that kills anyone whose name is written in it. He is pursued by the mysterious detective L while trying to create a perfect world without crime.

Jujutsu Kaisen centers on Yuji Itadori, who swallows a cursed object containing the finger of the powerful demon Ryomen Sukuna. He joins an organization of Jujutsu sorcerers who fight against Curses, evil spirits born from negative human emotions.

Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood tells the story of brothers Edward and Alphonse Elric who use alchemy to try bringing their mother back to life, but the attempt fails and costs Edward his arm and leg while Alphonse loses his entire body, forcing his soul to be bound to a suit of armor.

Spy x Family follows a spy named Loid Forger who must create a fake family for a mission. He adopts a telepathic daughter named Anya and marries a skilled assassin named Yor, neither knowing each other's true identities.
